What does it all mean?

Posted 09.21.15 / by Graham

Design is everywhere in our stupid lives. Good stuff, bad stuff and stuff that should have never been (i.e. shake weights). For those of us with half a brain and an ounce of passion, our ideas of what design can be motivating, aspirational or s

Being able to take ideas and transform them into something tangible is life-affirming. — MELINDA SANTILLIAN

Design is what we see. The chairs we sit in, the coffee cups we dump down our gullets, and even the shoes we wear rest on design and how it can solve your problem. It's a simple concept but not always simple in execution. Making something out of nothing is where the "magic" lies. So stay inspired. Don't take yourself seriously. Take design seriously. Because people are too damn used to the bad stuff. So let's make good stuff. That's all I have.

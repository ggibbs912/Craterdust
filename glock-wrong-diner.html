GLOCK "Wrong Diner"

The 'Wrong Diner' video below was one of a series produced for GLOCK featuring R. Lee Ermey as the Gunny. Working with Rogin Entertainment out of California, the director and director of photography furnished a loose script, a few photos of the diner and a basic shot list via email. After a few phone calls, the storyboards below are what was presented to the production company. With few differences, the video reflects a fairly accurate portrayal of the extended cut that can be seen currently on YouTube. The video featured here is the edited version which was created for commercial broadcasts. The 'Wrong Diner' is among the highest of GLOCK fan favorites and went on to win multiple Communicator awards the year it debuted.

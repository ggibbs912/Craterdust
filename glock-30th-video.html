GLOCK 30th Anniversary Video

Shot Show 2016: Las Vegas: Once again, GLOCK asked Craterdust to produce the annual Shot Show party along with a short film. Naturally, the film celebrated GLOCK's 30 year evolution and highlighted benchmark years in GLOCK's history. The story starts in the 80's and progresses through to the present, outlining GLOCK's strides in civilian, law enforcement and military markets.

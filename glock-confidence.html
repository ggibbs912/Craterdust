GLOCK Confidence Campaign

2013 was a record-setting year for GLOCK as they successfully shipped over a million pistols from their North American HQ in Smyrna, GA. With the upcoming launch of two new pistol models in January 2014, GLOCK was looking to refresh their advertising campaign creative while still maintaining the 'Confidence' messaging they have become synonymous with. CraterDust proposed a new campaign tagline that would leverage the previous years messaging while reaching out to the ever-growing numbers of concealed-carry pistol owners across the country. 'Confidence. It's What You Carry.' speaks to both the literal translation of what one carries on their person as well as to the way people carry themselves. The updated tagline allowed GLOCK to engage consumers on multiple levels and became the basis for the 2014 advertising campaign.

Print

As GLOCK has a long-standing relationship with most US gun enthusiast magazines and publications, it was no exception that 2014 would see many print ads run in a variety of sizes and formats. CraterDust worked with GLOCK and its partners to frame up the 2014 quarterly ad cycles and placements. We then proposed which key ads would make the biggest impact for each quarter based on the consumer outreach.

Digital

The 'Confidence' campaign continues through digital mediums via the GLOCK website and social media platforms such as Facebook and Twitter. While refreshing the current US website with new campaign imagery and design, we also provided creative direction for social activations to their millions of online fans.



Collateral

Created for consumer facing events, direct sales and fan engagement, GLOCK collateral such as patches, stickers and other takeaways were designed to bear the bold confidence messaging and stark brand-based imagery.


Apparel

GLOCK reached out to us to revamp their e-store with new designs for all their gear. Graphics were created by CraterDust to speak to the company's innovation and superior engineering while engaging their loyal audience of enthusiasts.
